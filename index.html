Disability Access Navigator

DAN helps you navigate California's Regional Center system -- what to ask for, how to ask for it, and what to do when the first answer is no. Free, confidential, available any time.

Before we start: DAN is an informational tool -- not legal advice. For your specific situation, you should seek guidance from a qualified advocate or attorney. Everything you share stays in this conversation -- nothing is stored or linked to you. Your feedback helps shape what DAN becomes.
→ DAN asks one question at a time. Click the button that fits — or type your own answer.
→ When the right next step is a written request, DAN drafts the email for you. Copy it and fill in the blanks.
→ Download your conversation before you close — nothing is saved between sessions.

By clicking Let's get started, you acknowledge that DAN provides general information only — not legal advice. DAN is not a lawyer or an authorized representative. For legal matters, consult a qualified attorney or disability rights organization.
