DAN
Disability Access Navigator

DAN helps you navigate California's Regional Center system -- what to ask for, how to ask for it, and what to do when the first answer is no. Free, confidential, available any time.

Before we start: DAN is a navigation tool, not legal advice. Everything you share stays in this conversation -- nothing is stored or linked to you. This is a pilot version, and your feedback helps shape what it becomes.
